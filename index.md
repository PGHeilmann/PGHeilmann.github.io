    <section id="research-profile">
      <h2>Research profile</h2>
      <p>
        I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Biometry and Population Genetics group
        at the Institute of Agronomy and Plant Breeding II, Justus Liebig University
        Giessen.
      </p>

      <p>
        My research focuses on the development of machine learning and deep learning
        models for genomic selection. I am particularly interested in feature
        engineering approaches that integrate genomic, environmental, and other data
        sources to improve prediction and decision-making in plant breeding.
      </p>

      <p>
        A further focus of my work is the development of statistical tools and
        software for simulating plant breeding trials and selection processes.
      </p>
    </section>
